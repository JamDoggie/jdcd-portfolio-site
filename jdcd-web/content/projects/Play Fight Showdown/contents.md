Play Fight: The Ultimate Showdown is a game **I began development on in Godot in 2023**. I spent over a year writing a custom server-authoritative networking system that works fantastic with many players and variable latency.

At its core, Play Fight is an arena shooter with one key difference: the ragdoll launch. Players can use their ragdoll launch to either hit players directly, or as mobility. Players also have a super bar that charges slowly and allows them to release a super ragdoll launch. In essence, the gameplay ends up being very similar to an arena shooter with some more dynamic elements.

This project is the creation of [the original Garry's Mod version](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2017752343) I started developing way back in 2018.
